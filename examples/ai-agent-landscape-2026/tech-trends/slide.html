Three forces reshape agent architecture in 2026

Trend 01
Multi-agent orchestration
Specialist agents coordinate via shared memory and handoff protocols. Single monolithic copilots lose to composable swarms in complex workflows — especially in ops, support, and research pipelines.
Trend 02
Tool-use standards
MCP and OpenAPI bridges normalize how agents call enterprise systems.
Trend 03
Eval & guardrails
Offline eval suites and runtime policy engines become purchase criteria.
