Two skills and template buckets form one pipeline

Orchestration
ppt skill
Clarifies intent, reads template index.json for story planning, initializes deck.json, delegates each page to authoring, runs screenshot visual QC, exports raster or editable PPTX via html2pptx-pro or pptxgenjs.
Authoring
ppt-html-authoring
Produces design.md + 1280×720 slide.html per outline entry.
Templates
ppt/assets/ buckets
corporate-light + claude-warm: 10 layouts with data-role slots.
